# PRODUCT.md — Patryk Zychowicz portfolio

register: brand

## Product purpose

A single-page portfolio positioning Patryk Zychowicz for the IT industry as an
AI-Native Developer & Product Builder. Its one job: prove, with a live shipped
product, that "directing AI to build production software" is real and rigorous.
The centerpiece is MGT Bookings, a reservation system in daily use at a real
restaurant.

## Users

Recruiters, hiring managers, and founders at EU (remote-friendly) companies:
AI-augmented dev teams, AI training/data-quality orgs, and especially
hospitality-tech / booking-platform companies. They skim for 30 seconds; the
site must land "live production app, real users, disciplined process" fast.

## Brand & tone

Honest, precise, quietly confident. The signature move is candor: the site says
plainly that Patryk directs AI tools rather than hand-writing algorithms, and
that this holds up under scrutiny. No hype words, no "passionate", no emoji.

## Visual world

Third member of the MGT app family: the site inherits their shared design
contract (6-stop glass gradient background, translucent cards, #007AFF accent,
SF-type system stack, light/dark theme via one data-theme attribute) but at
editorial portfolio scale. Glassmorphism here is purposeful brand continuity
with the apps being showcased, not decoration.

## Anti-references

- Generic AI-generated portfolio templates (purple gradients, sparkle emoji,
  "Crafting digital experiences").
- Agentic-AI aesthetic: neon glows, matrix vibes, terminal cosplay.
- Bootcamp-project galleries of dead demos. Every project shown must be real.

## Strategic principles

1. Claims come from `../my-page-context/Patryk_IT_Career_Positioning.md`; never
   invent achievements beyond it.
2. No raw email/phone in the page source (mailto assembled in JS at click time).
3. Zero dependencies, no build step: the repo doubles as proof of clean
   fundamentals for a learner-builder.
